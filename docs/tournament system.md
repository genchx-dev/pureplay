Full Technical & Business Explanation

Your platform has 2 tournament systems:

🏆 Pure Knockout Tournament
🧠 Swiss Hybrid Tournament

These two systems serve different purposes inside the platform.

🏆 1. PURE KNOCKOUT TOURNAMENT SYSTEM
🧠 WHAT IT IS

A knockout tournament is:

“Lose once and you are eliminated.”

Players continue advancing until:

1 player remains
🎯 PURPOSE OF KNOCKOUT MODE

This mode is designed for:

high pressure competition
fast tournaments
high-risk/high-reward gameplay
simpler tournament structure

Emotionally:

intense
dramatic
quick elimination
⚙️ HOW IT WORKS
📌 STEP 1 — PLAYER REGISTRATION

Example:

1,069 players register
📌 STEP 2 — SYSTEM CHECKS PLAYER COUNT

Knockout brackets require:

powers of 2

Examples:

64
128
256
512
1024
2048
❗ PROBLEM
1,069 ≠ power of 2

So system must reduce players.

📌 STEP 3 — PLAY-IN ROUND (QUALIFIER ROUND)

Nearest clean bracket:

1,024

Extra players:

1,069 - 1,024 = 45

System creates:

45 elimination spots
⚙️ HOW PLAY-IN WORKS

To eliminate 45 players:

90 players are selected
They play:
45 matches

Results:

45 winners survive
45 losers eliminated

Now:

1,024 players remain
📌 STEP 4 — MAIN KNOCKOUT BRACKET

Now tournament becomes clean:

Round 1	1,024

📌 STEP 5 — FINAL WINNER

Final match determines:

🥇 Winner
🥈 Runner-up
🏆 TOP 10 PAYOUT SYSTEM

Even though only 1 person wins tournament:

Platform rewards:

Top 10 players
⚙️ HOW TOP 10 IS CALCULATED
Automatic ranking logic:

Players ranked using:

Round reached
Win rate

Opponent strength (optional)

🧠 2. SWISS HYBRID TOURNAMENT SYSTEM
🧠 WHAT IT IS

Swiss Hybrid combines:

Swiss qualification + Knockout finals

Meaning:

Swiss ranking stage
Top players advance
Knockout decides final winner
🎯 PURPOSE OF SWISS HYBRID

Designed for:

fairness
player retention
engagement
longer tournament experience

Emotionally:

strategic
competitive
less punishing
⚙️ FULL STRUCTURE
📅 MONDAY–WEDNESDAY → SWISS STAGE

All players compete.

Example:

2,000 players
🎮 SWISS MATCH SYSTEM

Each player:

plays multiple matches
NOT eliminated immediately

Matches are:

BO3 (Best of 3)
⚙️ MATCHMAKING LOGIC

Players matched against:

similar-ranked players

Example:

3–0 players face other 3–0 players
2–1 players face other 2–1 players
📊 SCORING SYSTEM
Result	Points
Match win	+3
Match loss	0

Optional:

game differential bonus
streak bonuses
🎯 PURPOSE OF SWISS

Swiss stage:

filters strongest players
increases fairness
prevents instant elimination
📌 END OF SWISS STAGE

Wednesday night:

rankings lock
leaderboard generated
📌 QUALIFICATION

Example:

eliminated

single elimination bracket
